About Me

introduction

Greetings! My name is Philip. I am a Dutch 🇳🇱 second-year college student following the game development program at the HKU (Utrecht University of the Arts). I'm incredibly passionate about making games, creating music, drawing art and exploring new skills and knowledge.

Interests

I draw inspiration from the stylish aesthetics of the 30s, envisioning big band scenes where everyone wears hats. I'm particularly fascinated by games like Cuphead and manga like Spy x Family for their captivating art styles reminiscent of the 30-50s era. Outside my game development studies is music which I make in Fl Studio, my other big passion. I spend a lot of time composing and experimenting with different sounds. When it comes to art and design, I strive to create pieces that evoke joy, using vibrant colors and incorporating subtle humor. In terms of coding, I'm always seeking to enhance the modularity and efficiency of my code, often exploring design patterns to optimize my projects.

Learning Goals

I am eager to learn more about Godot, an open-source game engine. Its open-source nature fascinates me because I appreciate the community-driven aspect and the cost-free access, which is especially important for me as a student.
I want to enhance my ability to structure code better and make it more efficient. I believe this is crucial for becoming a better developer, as well-organized and efficient code makes development easier for both myself and others.
I am very interested in audio and music, and I want to become better at composing music that sounds professional. I also want to learn more about FMOD, a powerful tool for integrating audio in Unity.
