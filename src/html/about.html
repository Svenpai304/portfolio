About Me

Hello! This is Sven Wels, a video game programmer studying at the HKU currently in my second year. I was born in 2001 and have lived in Amsterdam-Zuidoost ever since.
A short and disastrous foray into mechanical engineering led me to try something I really wanted to do. I applied to the HKU, and got accepted into the Game Development program in 2022. I've felt more at home in this field than anywhere else, and the more I grow as a developer the more I enjoy making games.

Interests

What I like to spend my time doing the most is making things. That usually comes down to making video games or other digital artifacts, but I've ended up making a lot of electronic items over the past while and enjoyed that as well.

Broadly speaking, I love just about anything that really commit to its own personality- and the bolder the better. Striking visuals, unique music, stylish mechanics, they're easy ways to my heart.

Inspiration-wise, some of the works that have had the most impact on me are Kill Six Billion Demons, Chainsaw Man, Outer Wilds, ULTRAKILL, and Keep Your Hands Off Eizouken!

Learning Goals

I'm currently enjoying experimenting with Godot, an open-source game engine similar to Unity. Getting more broad experience across game engines is a good step in and of itself, but potentially being able to switch to an open-source engine for smaller full projects could be very valuable.
